<h4>Nb line max</h4>
Max number of lines in each field. Above that number, scrolling is used.<br>
<h4>First line height</h4>
Height of first line of each field (pixels).<br>
<h4>Line height</h4>
Height of other lines of each field (pixels).<br>
<h4>Button "Default height"</h4>
Reset fields "First line height" and "Line height".<br>
<h4>Automatic height</h4>
Adapt the height of field at each typed character.<br>
